# Palmoil-Investment-Analysis

Palm Oil Plantation Feasibility Analysis - Path to ₦1 Billion Annual Profit

Author: KaosaraO
Date: November 12, 2025

This is a web scraped analyzed data to know states palm oil investment will be profitable

This repository contains the financial modeling, assumptions, insights, and recommendations from a detailed feasibility study aimed at determining what it takes to build a palm oil production venture capable of generating ₦1 billion in annual profit in Nigeria.


1. Objectives

Determine the scale, location, and investment requirements needed to achieve ₦1B annual profit.

Use real industry benchmarks and multi-scenario modeling to assess profitability.

Provide state-by-state viability insights for commercial palm oil plantation development.


2.  Key Data Assumptions

Oil Extraction Rate (OER): 19%–23%

FFB Yield: 16–21 MT/ha

CPO Market Price: ₦480,000–₦560,000 per MT

Palm Kernel Yield & Prices: Included in model

Cost Structure: 12 major cost categories

Time to Productivity: 3-year maturation period

External Conditions: Stable rainfall + available labor

7.  Critical Success Factors

Achieving 21%+ oil extraction rate

Maintaining 18–21 MT/ha FFB yield

Competitive pricing and strong market access

Investing in modern processing facilities

Securing stable community partnerships


8. Conclusion

Achieving ₦1 billion annual profit from palm oil production is feasible with:

The right location,

The right scale,

And disciplined operational efficiency.


The optimistic scenario provides a high-return pathway, while a phased rollout reduces risk and validates all assumptions.
